Indian history and traditions are what makes us stand apart from the rest of the world. We all are tied to our interpersonal relationships through a special undefinable bond that we like to celebrate from time to time. We have a festival to celebrate every occasion and relation as well, that’s our glory. For instance, there’s Valentine's Day for lovers and Rakshabandhan to celebrate the bond of siblings. Likewise, we have boss’s day, father’s day, mother’s day, and many such that honor all relations respectively. For every festival, there’s a different story. What remains the same is the sense of belongingness and togetherness they bring. Whether you agree or not, they are imperative to stick around, especially in this busy schedule. To make these occasions extra special, we exchange gifts. The tradition has lived long for eternity and we do not want it to discontinue. The provision and acceptance of gifts represent the tenderness and regard we have for each other. Furthermore, it rebuilds our old relations and is a great start to begin a new one. It does not have to be a tangible gift, your efforts and accomplishments can also be a present to your loved ones. Psychologically, gifts are also proven to reduce anxiety, build confidence, and evoke gratitude. So what are you waiting for, do that all for your people, Treasure Gifty’s here!
